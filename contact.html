Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Brand identity projects typically take 4-6 weeks, website design 6-8 weeks, and video production 3-5 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

What's your design process like?

We follow a collaborative 5-phase process: Discovery (understanding your needs), Strategy (defining approach), Concept (initial ideas), Design (refinement), and Delivery (final assets). You're involved at every step with regular check-ins and feedback sessions.

Do you work with businesses of all sizes?

Absolutely! We've worked with startups, small businesses, and large enterprises. Our approach is scalable and tailored to your specific needs, budget, and goals regardless of company size.

What happens after project completion?

We provide all final files, brand guidelines (for identity projects), and offer ongoing support. Many clients choose our maintenance packages for websites or retainer services for ongoing creative needs.

Can you work with our existing team?

Yes! We're experienced in collaborating with in-house teams, other agencies, and freelancers. We can integrate seamlessly with your existing workflow and communication channels.
